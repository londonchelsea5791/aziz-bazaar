Our Story

Sourced with relationships, not invoices

Aziz Bazaar started with one simple idea: bring the real souk experience to London, without the markup of middlemen. We work directly with family workshops across Morocco — the same weavers, metalworkers, and potters whose craft has been passed down for generations.

Find us on Harrow Road, right next to Casbah Café — step inside and we're happy to tell you the story behind every piece on the shelf.
